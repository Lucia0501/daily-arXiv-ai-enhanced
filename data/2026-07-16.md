Method: 建立标量高斯隐蔽覆盖模型，Alice的低功率隐蔽信号叠加在可追踪的公共分量上；Willie利用所有物理可获取边信息减去公共分量的最优估计后，对残差施加相对熵约束（预算δ）；在平稳条件下推导最大可靠传输隐蔽有效载荷的平方根律常数；针对时变条件残差方差，提出一阶功率分配方案。

Result: 最大可靠传输隐蔽有效载荷为R_C^*√n(1+o(1))比特，其中R_C^*=(σ₀²/(σ_B² ln2))√δ，σ₀²=σ_W²+σ_e²。平方根律常数由Willie实际检测器输入的总方差决定。低功率高斯信号可达到该常数，匹配的逆命题证明一阶最优性。时变情况下，应分配更多隐蔽功率给更大残差方差。

Conclusion: Willie利用边信息抵消公共分量后的残差方差（包括接收机噪声和不可消除的抵消误差）是决定隐蔽通信容量的关键因素，而非仅接收机噪声。结果揭示了现实场景中隐蔽通信的平方根律常数的真实构成，为实际系统设计提供了理论指导。研究限于高斯后抵消残差和已知条件方差情形。

Abstract: Covert communication enables Alice to transmit to Bob while making the transmission difficult for Willie to detect. We study a scalar Gaussian covert-overlay model in which Alice's low-power covert signal is superimposed on an aggregate public component generated by Alice or other trackable sources. Willie is given all physically obtainable side information, including protocol details, timing, pilots, channel estimates, and calibration information, and subtracts his best estimate of the public component before testing. Covertness is imposed on the resulting residual through a relative-entropy constraint with budget $δ$ conditioned on Willie's side information. In the stationary case, the residual under no covert transmission has variance $σ_0^2=σ_W^2+σ_e^2$, where $σ_W^2$ is Willie's receiver-noise variance and $σ_e^2$ is the irreducible cancellation error. Over $n$ channel uses, the maximal reliably transmissible covert payload is $R_C^\star\sqrt{n}(1+o(1))$ bits, where $R_C^\star=\frac{σ_0^2}{σ_B^2\ln 2}\sqrtδ$, and $σ_B^2$ is Bob's receiver-noise variance. Thus, the square-root-law (SRL) constant is governed by the variance at Willie's actual detector input, not by receiver noise alone. Low-power Gaussian signaling achieves this constant, and a matching converse establishes first-order optimality within the conditioned additive Gaussian innovation model. For known time-varying conditioned residual variances, we also derive the first-order allocation, which assigns more covert power to larger residual variances. The results require a Gaussian post-cancellation null residual with known conditioned variance; non-Gaussian residuals and fixed non-vanishing variance uncertainty are outside the scope of this paper.
