*Junho Kweon,Vishal Monga*

Main category: eess.SP

TL;DR: 提出TIGRE算法，通过物理启发的正则化增强多路径环境下汽车雷达的鬼目标识别能力，特别在低信噪比条件下表现更优。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 汽车雷达在真实驾驶环境中常因护栏等表面的反射产生多路径传播，导致出现位置错误的鬼目标（虚假检测），这对自动驾驶导航构成挑战。现有MP-IAA方法在低信噪比环境下效果下降。

Method: 提出TIGRE算法，引入物理启发的正则化器捕捉多路径传播的结构模式，将其纳入估计成本形成新的损失函数。该算法迭代最小化正则化损失，并证明正则化器渐近地强制执行DOA/DOD网格上的L0稀疏性。

Result: 数值实验表明，该方法显著提高了各种多路径场景下的角度网格估计质量，特别是在低信噪比环境中，为后续鬼目标识别提供了更可靠的基础。

Conclusion: TIGRE算法通过物理启发的正则化增强了汽车雷达在多路径环境下的鲁棒性，特别是在低信噪比条件下，能更有效地识别鬼目标，提升自动驾驶系统的可靠性。

Abstract: This study presents a novel algorithm for identifying ghost targets in automotive radar by estimating complex valued signal strength across a two-dimensional angle grid defined by direction-of-arrival (DOA) and direction-of-departure (DOD). In real-world driving environments, radar signals often undergo multipath propagation due to reflections from surfaces such as guardrails. These indirect paths can produce ghost targets - false detections that appear at incorrect locations - posing challenges to autonomous navigation. A recent method, the Multi-Path Iterative Adaptive Approach (MP-IAA), addresses this by jointly estimating the DOA/DOD angle grid, identifying mismatches as indicators of ghost targets. However, its effectiveness declines in low signal-to-noise ratio (SNR) settings. To enhance robustness, we introduce a physics-inspired regularizer that captures structural patterns inherent to multipath propagation. This regularizer is incorporated into the estimation cost, forming a new loss function that guides our proposed algorithm, TIGRE (Target-Induced angle-Grid Regularized Estimation). TIGRE iteratively minimizes this regularized loss and we show that our proposed regularizer asymptotically enforces L0 sparsity on the DOA/DOD grid. Numerical experiments demonstrate that the proposed method substantially enhances the quality of angle-grid estimation across various multipath scenarios, particularly in low SNR environments, providing a more reliable basis for subsequent ghost target identification.

This is in contrast to corresponding NS-assisted non-causal CSIT setting where it was previously noted that the optimal probability of error cannot be further improved by providing the state to the receiver.

### [4] [The Arithmetic Singleton Bound on the Hamming Distances of Simple-rooted Constacyclic Codes over Finite Fields](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.11788)
*Li Zhu,Hongfeng Wu*

Main category: cs.IT

TL;DR: 本文为有限域上的简单根常循环码建立了新的汉明距离上界——算术Singleton界，通过MED表示将算术结构转化为最小距离的限制约束。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 传统Singleton界可能无法达到，但原因不一定是线性限制，而是底层代数障碍。需要系统地将算术结构转化为最小距离的限制约束，揭示Singleton界不可达性的代数本质。

Method: 引入多重等差(MED)表示的概念，将简单根多项式的定义集进行MED表示。证明每个MED表示都会诱导出最小距离的上界，最粗的表示对应经典Singleton界，最强的表示定义为算术Singleton界。

Result: 算术Singleton界总是至少与Singleton界一样紧，获得了严格更紧的精确判据。对于不可约常循环码，该界由$ω+1$显式给出，其中$ω$与$q$模多项式阶的根的阶密切相关。

Conclusion: 这项工作首次系统地将算术结构（通过MED表示）转化为最小距离的限制约束，揭示了Singleton界可能不可达的原因不是线性限制，而是底层代数障碍。

Abstract: This paper establishes a novel upper bound-termed the arithmetic Singleton bound-on the Hamming distance of any simple-root constacyclic code over a finite field. The key technical ingredient is the notion of multiple equal-difference (MED) representations of the defining set of a simple-root polynomial, which generalizes the MED representation of a cyclotomic coset. We prove that every MED representation induces an upper bound on the minimum distance; the classical Singleton bound corresponds to the coarsest representation, while the strongest among these bounds is defined as the arithmetic Singleton bound. It is shown that the arithmetic Singleton bound is always at least as tight as the Singleton bound, and a precise criterion for it to be strictly tighter is obtained. For irreducible constacyclic codes, the bound is given explicitly by $ω+1$, where $ω$ is a constant closely related to the order of $q$ modulo the radical of the polynomial order. This work provides the first systematic translation of arithmetic structure-via MED representations-into restrictive constraints on the minimum distance, revealing that the Singleton bound may be unattainable not because of linear limitations, but due to underlying algebraic obstructions.

### [5] [On Fundamental Limits of Transmission Activity Detection in Fluid Antenna Systems](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.11901)

  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 为流体天线系统和传统固定天线系统的传输活动检测建立统一的性能评估框架，解决活动检测中二进制状态参数难以直接应用CRB分析的问题

Method: 将二进制活动状态松弛为连续参数以适用CRB分析，针对协方差导向和相干两种检测模型推导闭式CRB表达式，对单天线FAS利用随机矩阵理论获得闭式相干CRB
